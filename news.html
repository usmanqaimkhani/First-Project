Epic Showdown Awaits: Barcelona vs. Real Madrid in the Spanish Super Cup 2025 Final

The stage is set for a thrilling El Clasico as FC Barcelona and Real Madrid lock horns in the Supercopa de España final for the third straight year. This high-stakes battle will unfold at the iconic King Abdullah Sports City Stadium in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, on Sunday, promising football fans a night to remember.

Barcelona secured their spot in the finale with a clinical 2-0 victory over Athletic Bilbao on Wednesday, while Real Madrid showcased their dominance with a commanding 3-0 triumph against Mallorca on Thursday.

Mark your calendars! The Spanish Super Cup 2025 final kicks off at 12:30 AM IST on Monday, January 13. Fans in India can catch all the action live via streaming.

Organized by the Royal Spanish Football Federation (RFEF) , the Supercopa de España is more than just a match—it’s the grand opening act of the Spanish football season, pitting the nation’s finest teams against each other in a battle for glory.

Get ready for a clash of titans as two of the greatest football clubs write the next chapter of their storied rivalry. Who will emerge victorious?

The participating clubs are:

  • La Liga champions
  • Copa del Rey winners
  • La Liga runners-up (if different from Copa del Rey finalists)
  • Copa del Rey runners-up

This year’s lineup features Real Madrid, reigning La Liga champions, and Barcelona, last season’s runners-up. Joining them are Athletic Bilbao, who lifted the Copa del Rey after defeating Mallorca, and Mallorca, the valiant runners-up.

A Glimpse at the Rivalry

The Supercopa is more than a trophy—it’s a prestigious opportunity for clubs to seize the season’s first silverware. Barcelona leads the all-time rankings with 14 titles, edging out Real Madrid by just one. Real, however, claimed last year’s title with a commanding 4-1 victory, while Barcelona reigned supreme in the previous edition with a 3-1 triumph over their eternal rivals.

Current Standings

As the tournament unfolds, both teams are in fierce form:

  • Real Madrid tops the La Liga standings with 43 points from 19 games.
  • Barcelona holds the third position, just five points adrift.

Who will add another chapter to their legacy? The stage is set, and the Supercopa de España is ready to crown its next champion.
