Explore the grandeur and history of the Olympic Games, featuring iconic sports, legendary athletes, and the host cities that brought the Olympics to life.

Summer Olympics

The Summer Olympics are the largest international sports event, featuring disciplines like athletics, swimming, gymnastics, and basketball.

  • Popular Sports: Athletics, Swimming, Gymnastics, Basketball, Football
  • First Edition: 1896 in Athens, Greece
  • Frequency: Every 4 years

Winter Olympics

The Winter Olympics celebrate sports on ice and snow, featuring events like skiing, ice skating, and hockey.

  • Popular Sports: Skiing, Snowboarding, Ice Hockey, Figure Skating
  • First Edition: 1924 in Chamonix, France
  • Frequency: Every 4 years
